## What Is ToToTel, Anyway?

ToToTel is a cloud VPS provider under VmShell INC, a US company established in 2021 with operations across Asia, the Americas, and Europe. They run 10Gbps data centers in Japan and the USA, and a CMI-1Gbps network in Hong Kong. All virtual servers run on the PVE (Proxmox Virtual Environment) management system — which is actually a pretty solid choice for flexibility and control.

The Hong Kong address registered is Room A17, 29/F, Ningjin Centre, Kwun Tong, Kowloon — they're not hiding who they are.

Their pitch is basically: stable, fast, global, with 24/7 Chinese-English support. That last bit matters a lot if you're based in Asia and need to actually talk to someone when things go sideways.

## PVE Management System — What Does That Mean for You?

All ToToTel servers run on Proxmox VE (PVE), which is a proper enterprise-grade hypervisor. In practical terms, this means you get a feature-rich management panel where you can handle snapshots, backups, console access (including noVNC and Xterm.js), and firewall configurations without needing to SSH into everything manually.

There's also a mobile app for management on the go — small detail, but it's handy when you just want to quickly reboot a stuck instance from your phone at 2am instead of hunting for a laptop.
